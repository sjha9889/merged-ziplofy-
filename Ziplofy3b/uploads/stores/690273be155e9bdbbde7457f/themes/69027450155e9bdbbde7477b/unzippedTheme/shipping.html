Important Delivery Information

Weather Conditions

Severe weather conditions such as storms, floods, or natural disasters may cause delivery delays beyond our control.

Customs Clearance

International shipments may experience delays due to customs clearance processes, which vary by destination country.

Festive Seasons

During peak seasons and festivals, delivery times may be extended due to high volume and courier availability.

Remote Locations

Deliveries to remote or difficult-to-access locations may require additional time and may incur extra charges.

Our Commitment: While we cannot control external factors, we commit to keeping you informed of any delays and working with our partners to minimize impact on your delivery.
