Shipping & Delivery

Safe & timely delivery, always

Introduction

At ORNATIVA, we are dedicated to providing a seamless and secure delivery experience for your exquisite jewelry. This policy outlines our shipping procedures, areas, and estimated timeframes.

Shipping Areas

Domestic Shipping

We proudly ship across all states within [Your Country].

International Shipping

We offer worldwide shipping to select countries. Availability and costs will be confirmed at checkout.

Delivery Timeframes

Order Processing: All orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

Estimated Delivery:

  • Standard Domestic: 5-7 business days
  • Express Domestic: 2-3 business days
  • International: 7-21 business days (depending on destination and customs clearance)

Shipping Charges

  • Domestic Standard: $5.00, or FREE for orders over $100.
  • Domestic Express: $15.00 flat rate.
  • International: Calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

Order Tracking

A tracking number will be provided via email once your order has been dispatched. You can track your order's journey on our Order Tracking page or directly through the courier's website.

Delays / Disclaimer

While we strive for timely delivery, ORNATIVA is not responsible for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances such as weather, courier issues, or customs processing for international orders. Any estimated delivery times are guides only.
