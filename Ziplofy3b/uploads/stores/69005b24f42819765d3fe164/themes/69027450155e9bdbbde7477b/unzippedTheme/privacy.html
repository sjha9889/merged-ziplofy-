Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us

At ORNATIVA, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and luxurious shopping experience. Your privacy is as precious to us as the jewelry we craft, and we handle your data with the utmost care and respect.

Information We Collect

Personal Details

Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address to process and deliver your orders.

Payment Information

Securely processed payment details through encrypted gateways. We never store your complete card information.

Browsing Data

Cookies, device information, and browsing patterns to enhance your shopping experience.

Communication

Messages, reviews, and feedback you share with us to improve our services and products.

How We Use Your Information

Process & Deliver Orders

To handle your purchases from payment processing to doorstep delivery.

Personalize Shopping Experience

To recommend jewelry that matches your style preferences and create a tailored shopping journey.

Send Offers & Newsletters

To keep you updated on new collections, exclusive offers, and jewelry care tips (with easy opt-out option).

Fraud Prevention & Security

To protect your account and transactions from fraudulent activities and maintain a secure platform.

Sharing of Information

Trusted Partners Only

We share your information only with trusted partners including secure payment gateways, reliable courier services, and verified service providers who help us deliver your orders.

We Never Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your trust is invaluable to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our customers and business.

Data Protection & Security

Your data security is our top priority. We employ industry-leading security measures to protect your personal and payment information.

256-bit SSL Encryption
Secure Cloud Servers
PCI DSS Compliant
Regular Security Audits

Bank-Level Security

Your data is protected with the same security standards used by financial institutions

Cookies Policy

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation, access to secure areas, and shopping cart functionality. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Authentication and security
  • Shopping cart management
  • Form submission and validation
  • Session management

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use Google Analytics and similar tools to improve our website performance.

  • Google Analytics tracking
  • Page performance monitoring
  • User behavior analysis
  • Traffic source identification

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements. They help us show you jewelry that matches your interests and preferences.

  • Personalized advertisements
  • Retargeting campaigns
  • Social media integration
  • Campaign effectiveness tracking
