Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and overnight shipping delivers the next business day. Free shipping is available on orders over $100.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Contact us for specific shipping information to your country.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, our technical support team is available Monday-Friday 9AM-6PM PST. We provide support for setup, troubleshooting, and general product questions.
