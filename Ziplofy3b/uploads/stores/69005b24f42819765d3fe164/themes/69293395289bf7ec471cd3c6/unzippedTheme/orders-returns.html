Order & Return Policy

We built our policies to be transparent and simple so you can shop with confidence.

Order processing

  • Orders placed before 4 PM ship the same day from the nearest warehouse.
  • Once dispatched, tracking details are shared via SMS and email.
  • Need edits? Use the track order page within 30 minutes of placing an order.

Returns & replacements

Eligible products can be returned within 10 days of delivery if they are unused, with original packaging, and include all accessories. Electronics come with brand warranties and are eligible for replacement in case of manufacturing defects.

How to initiate a return

  1. Visit track-order.html and enter your order ID.
  2. Select the item, choose a reason, and upload supportive photos if applicable.
  3. Our team will schedule a pickup or share a drop-off location within 24 hours.

Refund timelines

Refunds are processed within 2 business days after quality inspection. Bank transfers or card payments may take 3-5 additional business days to reflect.

Need help?

Reach our resolution desk via help center or call +021-95-51-84 with your order number.
