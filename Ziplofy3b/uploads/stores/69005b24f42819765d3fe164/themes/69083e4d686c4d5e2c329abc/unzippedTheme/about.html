Our Story

Timeless Elegance Since 1995

Three decades of crafting exquisite jewelry that tells your unique story

Crafted with Passion

For over three decades, ORNATIVA has been synonymous with exceptional craftsmanship and timeless elegance. Founded in 1995 by master jeweler Rajesh Kumar, our brand began as a small workshop in Mumbai's historic jewelry district, where every piece was handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail.

Today, we continue this legacy of excellence, combining traditional techniques with modern innovation. Our artisans, many of whom have been with us for over 20 years, bring generations of expertise to every creation, ensuring that each piece tells a story of heritage, passion, and uncompromising quality.
