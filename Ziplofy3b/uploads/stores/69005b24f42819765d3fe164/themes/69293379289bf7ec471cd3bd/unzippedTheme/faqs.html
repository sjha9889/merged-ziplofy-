Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about shopping at ShopHub

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, or cash on delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, digital wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways. We never store your complete payment details.

Will I receive an invoice?

Yes, you'll receive a detailed invoice via email after your order is confirmed. You can also download it from your account.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and same-day delivery options are also available in select areas.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to all major cities in India. International shipping is available for select products. Please contact us for more details.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order in real-time through our website or mobile app.

What are the shipping charges?

Standard shipping is free on orders above ₹500. Express shipping costs ₹99, and same-day delivery costs ₹199 in select cities.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery. Products must be unused, in original packaging with tags attached. Free returns available for orders above ₹500.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes, we offer exchanges for size, color, or style within 7 days of delivery. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Account & Support

How do I create an account?

Click on "Account" in the top menu, then select "Register" to create a new account. You can also sign up during checkout.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email. You'll receive instructions to reset your password.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email at info@example.com, call us at +012 345 67890, or use the contact form on our Contact Us page. We're available 24/7 to assist you.

How do I add items to my wishlist?

Simply click the heart icon on any product page or product card to add it to your wishlist. You can view all wishlist items from the "Wishlist" link in the top menu.
