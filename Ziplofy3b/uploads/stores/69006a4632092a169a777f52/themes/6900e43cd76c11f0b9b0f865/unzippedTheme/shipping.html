Address Accuracy

Ensuring successful delivery

Customer Responsibility

Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate. We recommend:

  • Double-checking the address before placing your order
  • Including apartment/unit numbers and building names
  • Providing a contact phone number for delivery coordination
  • Using a business address if residential delivery is not available

Address Issues

Incorrect or incomplete addresses may result in:

  • Delivery delays or failed delivery attempts
  • Additional shipping charges for re-routing
  • Package return to sender
  • Potential restocking fees for returned items
