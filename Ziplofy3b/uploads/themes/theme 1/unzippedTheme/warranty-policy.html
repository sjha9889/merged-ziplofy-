What's Covered

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and normal wear under proper use conditions.

Manufacturing Defects

  • Hardware malfunctions
  • Component failures
  • Assembly defects
  • Material defects

Battery Issues

  • Battery degradation
  • Charging problems
  • Power management issues
  • Battery replacement

Connectivity Problems

  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Bluetooth issues
  • Network problems
  • Signal strength

Audio/Video Issues

  • Speaker problems
  • Display issues
  • Camera malfunctions
  • Audio quality

What's Not Covered

Certain conditions and damages are not covered under our warranty policy.

User Damage

  • Physical damage from drops or impacts
  • Water damage or liquid exposure
  • Damage from improper use
  • Cosmetic damage from normal wear

Modifications

  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Third-party software installation
  • Hardware modifications
  • Jailbreaking or rooting

Environmental

  • Extreme temperature exposure
  • Exposure to dust or sand
  • Power surge damage
  • Natural disasters
