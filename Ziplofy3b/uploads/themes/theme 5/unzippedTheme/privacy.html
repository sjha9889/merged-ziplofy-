Your privacy matters

This policy describes how Ectasell collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website or purchase with us.

Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, phone number, shipping addresses.
  • Payment data: credit/debit details are processed securely via PCI-compliant gateways; we never store full card numbers.
  • Usage data: device identifiers, pages visited, and interactions that help us improve the experience.

How we use your data

We use information to fulfil orders, deliver personalized recommendations, communicate order status, and prevent fraud. With explicit consent we send promotional offers which you can opt out of anytime.

Data security & retention

We apply TLS encryption, regional hosting, and routine audits. Records are retained only for as long as required for legal compliance and customer support.

Cookies & tracking

Cookies help us remember cart items and provide relevant content. You can adjust preferences via your browser without affecting access to our core services.

Your choices

  • Download or delete your data by emailing privacy@ectasell.com.
  • Unsubscribe instantly from marketing messages using the footer link in any email.
  • Disable optional cookies via your browser or within account settings.

Questions about privacy? Our compliance team responds within 48 hours.
