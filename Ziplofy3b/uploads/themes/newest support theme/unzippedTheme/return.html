Items Not Eligible for Return

While we strive to accommodate all return requests, certain items cannot be returned due to their personalized nature or hygiene considerations.

Personalized & Custom Jewelry

Items with custom engravings, personalized designs, or made-to-order pieces cannot be returned as they are specifically crafted for individual customers.

Resized Jewelry

Jewelry that has been resized, modified, or altered in any way after purchase cannot be returned due to the permanent changes made to the piece.

Sale & Clearance Items

Items purchased during special sales, clearance events, or with significant discounts are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Expired Return Period

Items beyond the 30-day return window cannot be accepted for return unless there are exceptional circumstances or product defects.
