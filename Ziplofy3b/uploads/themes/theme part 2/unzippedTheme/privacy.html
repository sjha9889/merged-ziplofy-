Privacy Policy

Your trust is our most precious jewel.

At LUXORA, we respect your privacy and protect your personal information with utmost care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through encrypted channels)
  • Account preferences and communication settings

Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Website usage patterns and preferences
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

Order Processing

Process and fulfill your jewelry orders, manage shipping and returns

Account Management

Create and maintain your account, provide customer support

Communication

Send order updates, promotional offers, and important notifications

Analytics

Improve our website, products, and services based on usage patterns

Sharing of Information

Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, and delivering products to you.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

Consent

We will not share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality, shopping cart, and user authentication.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve user experience.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

Your Rights

Access

Request access to your personal data we hold

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data

Objection

Object to processing of your personal data

Portability

Request transfer of your data to another service

Restriction

Request restriction of processing activities

Data Security

SSL Encryption

All data transmission is protected by industry-standard SSL encryption

Secure Servers

Your data is stored on secure, monitored servers with regular backups

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data

Payment Security

Payment information is processed through PCI DSS compliant systems
