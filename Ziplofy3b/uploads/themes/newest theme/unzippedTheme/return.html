Return & Refund Policy

We want every purchase to sparkle with confidence.

At LUXORA, we value your trust. If you're not fully satisfied with your jewelry purchase, our simple return & refund process ensures peace of mind. We've designed our policy to be transparent, fair, and customer-friendly.

Eligibility for Returns

Understanding what qualifies for returns

Timeframe

Returns must be initiated within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact.

Condition Requirements

Jewelry must be unworn and in original packaging. All certificates, authenticity cards, and protective materials must be included.

Documentation

Original receipt or order confirmation is required. Returns without proper documentation may be subject to delays or rejection.

Non-Returnable Items

Items that cannot be returned

Personalized Items

Custom engravings, personalized messages, or made-to-order pieces cannot be returned due to their unique nature.

Clearance Items

Items purchased during clearance sales or special promotions are final sale and cannot be returned.

Worn Items

Any jewelry that shows signs of wear, damage, or has been altered cannot be accepted for return.

Gift Items

Items purchased as gifts may have different return policies. Please contact customer service for assistance.

Return Process

Simple steps to return your purchase

1

Request Return

Contact our customer service team via email or phone to initiate your return request. Provide your order number and reason for return.

2

Get Approval

We'll review your request and send you a return authorization number (RMA) along with detailed return instructions.

3

Package & Ship

Securely package your item with all original materials and ship it back using the provided return label or your preferred carrier.

4

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Refunds

How your refund is processed

Payment Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the purchase. Credit card refunds appear within 3-5 business days, while bank transfers may take 5-10 business days.

Processing Time

Once your return is received and approved, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is initiated.

Refund Amount

You'll receive a full refund for the item price. Return shipping costs may be deducted unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Exchanges

Exchange for a different size or style

Size Exchanges

We offer free size exchanges for rings and bracelets within 14 days of delivery. Simply contact us with your preferred size.

Style Exchanges

Exchange for a different style of equal or greater value. Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly.

Exchange Process

Follow the same return process, but specify "exchange" instead of "refund" when contacting customer service.

Exchange Timeline

Exchanges are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your returned item.

Shipping Costs

Understanding return shipping fees

Free Return Shipping

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong item sent by mistake
  • Size exchanges (rings & bracelets)
  • Items over $500 value

Customer Pays Shipping

  • Change of mind returns
  • Style exchanges
  • Items under $500 value
  • Returns after 14 days

Note: Return shipping costs are typically $8-15 depending on the item size and weight. We'll provide you with the exact cost when processing your return request.
