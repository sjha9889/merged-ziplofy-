Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders over $500. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee. All jewelry is carefully packaged in our signature luxury boxes.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn jewelry in its original condition. Custom pieces and engraved items are final sale. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. We provide a prepaid return label for your convenience.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Store your jewelry in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Clean with a soft, dry cloth and avoid contact with perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals. For diamonds and precious stones, use a mild soap solution and soft brush. Professional cleaning is recommended annually.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes! Our master artisans can create custom pieces tailored to your specifications. We offer consultation services to bring your vision to life. Custom pieces typically take 4-6 weeks to complete and require a 50% deposit to begin production.

What certifications do your diamonds have?

All our diamonds are certified by GIA (Gemological Institute of America) and come with detailed certificates. We only source conflict-free diamonds and precious stones. Each piece includes a certificate of authenticity and warranty information.
