Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us

Introduction

At ORNATIVA, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring a safe and luxurious shopping experience. This policy details how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Details

Name, Email, Shipping Address, Billing Address, Phone Number.

Payment Information

Securely processed via trusted third-party payment gateways; we do not store sensitive payment data.

Browsing Information

IP address, device type, browser information, pages visited, time spent, and cookies data.

How We Use Your Information

Order Processing & Delivery

To fulfill and deliver your purchases, send order confirmations, and provide customer support.

Personalized Shopping Experience

To tailor product recommendations, display relevant content, and remember your preferences.

Marketing & Communication

To send you newsletters, promotional offers, and updates (with clear opt-out options).

Fraud Prevention & Security

To protect our website, customers, and prevent fraudulent transactions.

Sharing of Information

We value your privacy. Your information is shared only with trusted third-party partners essential for our services, such as secure payment gateways and reliable courier services. We strictly never sell your personal data to any third parties.

Data Protection & Security

We implement robust security measures, including advanced encryption technologies, secure servers, and SSL certificates, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Cookies Policy

These cookies are vital for the basic functionality of our website, enabling core features like security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these.

We use analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve performance and user experience. These cookies collect anonymous data.

Marketing cookies are used to track visitors across websites. The intention is to display ads that are relevant and engaging for the individual user and thereby more valuable for publishers and third-party advertisers.

Your Rights

  • Access and Rectification: You have the right to request access to and correction of your personal data.
  • Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.
  • Object to Processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes.
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.
